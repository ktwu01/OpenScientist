## Fact
Test-Time Reinforcement Learning (TTRL) allows a model to self-improve at inference with no explicit labels — the model generates multiple candidate answers, uses a self-consistency or majority signal as reward, and updates on-the-fly. Concrete result: Qwen-2.5-Math-7B pass@1 on AIME 2024 improved ~211% under TTRL. MATTRL is the multi-agent extension that injects structured experience into inference-time negotiation among agents. TTRL is one of the strongest existing empirical arguments that the "agentic RL" and "test-time reasoning" research directions are converging into a single paradigm, not two.
