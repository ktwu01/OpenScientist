## Situation
Hydrological model outputs match precipitation inputs but fail to reproduce seasonal persistence in water fluxes.

## Action
Compared modeled vs. observed seasonal decay patterns rather than point accuracy.

## Outcome
Identified missing storage component (rock moisture) rather than parameter error.

## Lesson
**Temporal structure mismatch signals missing physics**, not calibration error.

## Retrieval Cues
- Correct short-term response but incorrect long-term decay.
- Persistent evapotranspiration in dry periods not captured.
