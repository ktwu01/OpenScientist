## Situation
Persistent anxiety, fatigue, and physiological symptoms during research period.

## Action
Continued attempting cognitive work without adjusting physiological state.

## Outcome
Reduced performance, increased instability, no improvement in research output.

## Lesson
Cognitive performance depends on physiological stability; ignoring this creates **false research bottlenecks**.

## Retrieval Cues
- Repeated inability to focus despite clear tasks.
- Physical symptoms co-occurring with cognitive slowdown.
