## When
Progress stalls despite available expertise (e.g., senior lab members), with prolonged independent attempts and no external input.

## Decision
Preferred: Force external consultation after a fixed time threshold (e.g., 3–5 days without progress).

Rejected:
- Continuing solo exploration indefinitely → no new information enters system.
- Waiting for formal deadlines → delays feedback loop.

Reasoning:
Stall arises from **closed information loop**, not lack of ability.

## Local Verifiers
- Repeated re-derivation of same idea.
- No new references, datasets, or methods introduced over multiple sessions.

## Failure Handling
If consultation does not resolve:
- Ask for **specific failure modes**, not general advice.
- Convert vague problem into testable sub-questions before re-engaging.

## Anti-exemplars
- Early-stage exploration where independence is still generating new hypotheses.
