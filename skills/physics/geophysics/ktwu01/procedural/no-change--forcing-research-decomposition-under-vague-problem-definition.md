## When
Problem feels large, vague, or undefined; no clear next step.

## Decision
Preferred: Convert into **binary testable sub-questions**.

Rejected:
- Literature browsing without hypothesis → passive consumption.
- Brainstorming without constraints → no convergence.

Reasoning:
Progress requires **decision points**, not idea accumulation.

## Local Verifiers
- Each sub-question answerable with data or calculation.
- Each answer reduces uncertainty space.

## Failure Handling
If decomposition fails:
- Anchor on observable quantities (data, measurements).
- Start from contradiction between model and observation.

## Anti-exemplars
- Well-defined optimization problems with clear objective functions.
