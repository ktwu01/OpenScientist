## Fact
Lab-level hidden variables (advisor behavior, favoritism, publication bottlenecks) significantly influence research output and career trajectory.

## Evidence
Repeated emphasis on:
- Graduation delays tied to advisor policies.
- Unequal resource allocation among students.
- Weekend meetings and control dynamics.

## Expiry Signal
If institutional transparency increases (e.g., open lab metrics, enforced policies), impact may decrease.
