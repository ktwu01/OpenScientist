## Fact
In modern land surface modeling synthesis, benchmarking frameworks are a primary organizing axis of the field, not optional supporting examples. Site-scale protocols such as PLUMBER/PLUMBER2 and larger-scale frameworks such as ILAMB and LS3MIP help define how models are compared across scales.
