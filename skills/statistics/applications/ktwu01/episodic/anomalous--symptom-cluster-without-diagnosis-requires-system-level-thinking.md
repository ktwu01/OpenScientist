## Skill 6

**Skill Name:** symptom-cluster-without-diagnosis-requires-system-level-thinking

**Memory Type:** episodic
**Subtype:** anomalous

## Situation

Multiple physiological symptoms:

* low potassium
* low lymphocytes
* nasal congestion
* abdominal pain
* orthostatic hypotension
* anxiety

Standard tests (bloodwork, imaging) show no major pathology.

## Action

Observed mismatch between:

* objective test results
* subjective severe symptoms

## Outcome

Indicated potential **system-level dysregulation** (e.g., stress-driven, autonomic, or multi-factor interaction) rather than single-disease explanation.

## Implication

Standard diagnostic pipelines may fail when:

* multiple weak signals combine
* no single dominant pathology exists

## Lesson

IF multiple moderate anomalies appear across systems without a unifying diagnosis, THEN investigate systemic interactions instead of isolated causes.

## Retrieval Cues

* Multi-system symptoms
* Normal standard diagnostics
* Persistent unexplained distress
