## Skill 1

**Skill Name:** overconfidence-induced-research-stall

**Memory Type:** procedural
**Subtype:** no-change

## When

IF a researcher works independently on an open-ended problem (e.g., model development, hydrology integration, or new concept synthesis like rock moisture + Noah-MP) AND does not consult senior collaborators despite availability AND progress stalls without clear hypotheses.

## Decision

Force early external consultation (e.g., senior lab member, advisor-guided pairing), instead of continuing solo iteration.

Rejected:

* Continuing independent exploration → leads to indefinite delay without feedback loop.
* Waiting for “complete idea” before asking → increases latency and error accumulation.

## Why

The bottleneck is not lack of intelligence but lack of **external constraint and feedback**. Without deadlines or critique:

* Hypothesis space expands uncontrollably
* No pruning mechanism
* No validation signal

This creates a **false sense of progress with zero convergence**.

## Local Verifiers

* > 1 week without producing a concrete result or falsifiable hypothesis
* No intermediate outputs shown to others
* Increasing internal confidence but no external validation
* Tasks remain “in progress” without measurable milestones

## Anti-exemplars

* Clearly scoped tasks with defined benchmarks (e.g., reproduce paper result)
* Situations where external input is unavailable or low quality
